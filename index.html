I am a third-year Ph.D. student at University of Oxford supervised by Prof. Christian Rupprecht and Prof. Philip Torr. I am a part of both the Visual Geometry Group as well as Torr Vision Group.

I like building multi-modal generative models and (self-)supervised generalist models. I had fun researching artificial intelligence at Stability AI (2024), Google (2023), Microsoft (2022), ByteDance (2021), JHU (2021), and SenseTime (2020).
